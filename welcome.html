Flow

How BillMyFlat fits into your monthly cycle

Simple for committees, transparent for residents, and structured enough for auditors.

Step 1

Set up your society

Add buildings, flats, owners and tenants. Configure your billing logic — fixed per flat, per sq. ft., parking, sinking fund, or any custom head.

Step 2

Generate & share bills

With a single action, generate invoices for the entire society. Residents receive a clear breakdown with due dates and payment options.

Step 3

Track payments in real time

See who has paid, what's pending, and auto-trigger reminders for dues. Export clean reports at month-end for books and meetings.

Designed around transparency & trust

Every rupee is traceable. Every resident sees the same numbers as the committee.

  • Consistent, system-generated invoices instead of ad-hoc Excel sheets.
  • Clear mapping of each payment to a flat, invoice and month.
  • Audit-ready reports that help during society elections and AGMs.
  • Built to reduce disputes around "who paid what & when".
